Solar Subclass

Light Based Subclass

Sometimes the only answer is to burn it all away

- Destiny 2 Grimoire

Solar energy is related to combustion, heat and nuclear fusion, and can be wielded by Guardians to generate flames and other heat-related phenomena. Solar requires a person to both exploit and rely on the power of our sun (and suns/stars in general), yet to master it requires them to understand the star's duality; that the nuclear force that scorches anything foolish enough to draw near is the same force we harness to heal. It feeds the flora of the galaxy, just as it begets drought and famine. In the end, destruction and re-creation are a necessary cycle
